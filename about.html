About Our Advocacy

Know the FACTS, Protect Yourself

Educate Teenagers about the Consequences of Unsafe Sex

We are committed to advocating for your well-being and amplifying your voice. Our mission is to promote safe sexual practices and empower individuals with the knowledge and support necessary to make informed decisions about their health. We aim to advocate for and promote safe sexual practices, particularly given the widespread lack of awareness regarding sexual health and the potential consequences of unsafe sexual behavior. As Senior High School students of Caloocan City Business High School, we strive to raise awareness about the risks associated with unsafe sex and promote safer alternatives.
