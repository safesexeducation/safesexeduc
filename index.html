CONSEQUENCES OF UNSAFE SEX

Unsafe sex, also known as unprotected sex, carries a multitude of risks that can have significant consequences for both physical and mental well-being.

Visit us to know more

Alternatives To Use

We offer this alternatives for a safe sex

Calendar Method

The calendar method, also known as the rhythm method, is a natural family planning method that helps identify your fertile days based on your menstrual cycle.

Condoms

Condoms are an essential tool for safe and responsible sexual health.

DON'T use Contraceptive Pills

Contraceptive pills are a highly effective method of birth control. They're not suitable for everyone.

Our Wants

Here are our following objectives for making this advocacy website

To Spread Awareness About The Consequences of Unsafe Sex

We aim to disseminate information regarding the repercussions of unprotected sexual activity to raise awareness about the potential consequences.

Uphold Alternative Methods of Safe Sexual Practices

We advocate for the utilization of the calendar method and condoms to prevent unwanted pregnancies and sexually transmitted infections.

Foster Open Communication

Open communication is crucial for couples to maintain sexual health. It allows for honest discussions about STDs, preventing transmission, and fostering a healthier relationship.
